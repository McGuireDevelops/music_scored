# Roadmap and priorities

## Differentiator (keep)

Learning Scores stays **human teacher–led**:

- Teachers lead curriculum, assessment, and feedback.
- AI assists teachers only (analysis, lesson summaries); no learner-facing AI tutor.
- Domain depth: film music, MediaReference, structured rubrics and quizzes.
